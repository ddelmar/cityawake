City Awake’s Coalition and Festival Partners

The City Awake Coalition is comprised of nearly 250 social impact organizations, from non-profits and innovative social enterprises to academic institutions. We convene and collaborate with them to make Boston a global hub for social impact. Most of our coalition members are running events during the 2015 Festival, although it’s not required. If your organization is interested in participating in the Festival but has not joined the coalition yet, we invite you to do so (we’ve made it easy with a 2-in-1 registration form below).

Submitting Your Event for the Festival

Once you complete the brief Festival Partner Registration form below, we will match your organization with a City Awake liaison, who will be your guide for all things Festival. Within a week, we’ll set you up with your own project-management tool, with access to all the cool (and useful) resources we’ve developed to support your organization’s event.

Important Festival Dates & Deadlines

Coordinating hundreds of events and organizations can get tricky, so we’ve set September 25th as the deadline to add your organization’s event to the Festival lineup. Don’t panic, we’re here to help! Just complete the quick partner registration form as soon as you can to get the ball rolling.

In the meantime, make sure to join us on September 8th for the Festival Partners Open House!

Resources City Awake Can Provide

We’ve learned a lot in the past year, and we want to share some of those lessons with you! From marketing and communication strategies to tools and templates, your City Awake liaison will help you through the painless on-boarding process and provide resources from marketing and communication strategies to tools and templates to help you plan your organization’s Festival event.

How Your Oganization Can Participate

Organize an event. Your organization will lead the planning of an impact-focused event that takes place within the Festival window (November 5-14). It can be as simple as a happy hour or ambitious as a summit. It's entirely up to you. Were you already planning an event in November? It must be fate!


Collaborate on an event. Don’t feel like you have the capacity to design an event on your own? Or maybe you have an idea but aren’t sure how to move it forward? No problem, we love when organizations collaborate! The City Awake team will do it’s best to match your organization will another one that is also interested in collaboration. Just use the same registration form below to get started.


Provide in-kind support. We get it; organizing an event is a lot of work! So if that’s not an option for you this year, don’t fret, there are other ways to get involved! We’re always grateful to accept in-kind donations to the festival and events, from providing venue space or catering for an event, to lending your professional expertise.


Have another idea? Just let us know by completing the registration form below!

The City Hall Talks

“City Hall Talks” is a new series we’re trying out during the 2015 Festival to provide a dedicated platform to contribute to the Imagine Boston 2030 citywide plan. City Awake will match organizations to design and moderate a “City Hall Talks” event, with a new thematic discussion happening daily from November 9 – 13. If your organization is interested in collaborating on this initiative with us, or if you have questions, reach out to us using the form below!

Charging Admission for Your Event

We realize running an event has costs, in time, resources, and staff, and we absolutely do not want participation in the festival to be a burden upon any organization. With that in mind, if offering free admission to your event is not possible, we encourage organizations to consider charging a modest fee to cover operational costs, in an effort to make the festival as a whole accessible to all!
